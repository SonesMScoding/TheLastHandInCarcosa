Hastur's Baccarat: Rulebook

1. Game Overview

Hastur's Baccarat is a game of chance, strategy, and cosmic intrigue. Players wager on the outcome of a contest between the Player and the Banker, with the mysterious forces of Carcosa offering boons and blessings to those who dare.

2. Gameplay Flow

  1. Activate Boons & Review Items: Before betting, you may activate up to three Boons and review your Blessings (items).
  2. Place Bets: Choose to bet on Player, Banker, or Tie. Only one zone may be bet on per round.
  3. Deal Cards: Two cards are dealt to both Player and Banker.
  4. Natural Win: If either side has a total of 8 or 9, the round ends immediately.
  5. Third Card Rule: If no natural win, a third card may be drawn for Player and/or Banker according to the rules below.
  6. Use Items: If you possess any Blessings (items), you may use them at this stage to influence the round.
  7. End Round: The winner is determined. Payouts are made, the Ledger of Carcosa is updated, and you may prepare for the next round.

4. Third Card Rule

Player's Third Card

  • If the Player's total is 0-5, Player draws a third card.
  • If the Player's total is 6 or 7, Player stands.

Banker's Third Card

The Banker draws a third card based on their own total and the Player's third card (if drawn):

  • If Banker total is 0-2: Banker draws a third card.
  • If Banker total is 3-6: Banker draws depending on the value of Player's third card.
  • If Banker total is 7: Banker stands.
  • If either side has a natural (8 or 9), no third card is drawn.

See in-game tooltips for a detailed third card chart.

5. Player Mechanics

  • Shoe: Click the shoe to view remaining cards.
  • Ledger of Carcosa: Track your game history and results.
  • Clear Bet: Use this to change your bet or placement before dealing.
  • Boons: Click to activate/deactivate up to three boons at a time.
  • Blessings (Items): Click an item and then Use to activate its effect.
  • End Round: Ends the round, pays out, and allows for player upkeep.

6. Boons

Boons are powerful advantages that can be activated before each round. You may have several unlocked, but only three can be active at a time. Boons provide unique effects to aid you in your struggle against fate. Activate or deactivate them while the table is clear.

7. Blessings (Items)

Blessings are one-time-use items granted when certain conditions are met. Each item has a unique effect and can only be used once per game. To use a Blessing, select it from the Blessings box and click Use. Item descriptions explain their effects and timing.

8. Win & Lose Conditions

  • Win: Amass wealth, unlock all boons and blessings, and survive the shifting tides of Carcosa.
  • Lose: If your funds reach zero, your journey ends. The Yellow King awaits your return...
May the stars align in your favor.
